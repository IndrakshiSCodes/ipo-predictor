How it works

ListIQ predicts the listing day gain percentage for Indian IPOs using historical subscription and grey market data. The dominant signal is grey market premium — GMP accounts for 76% of the model's predictive power. Subscription data (QIB, NII, retail) contributes the remaining signal.


Data sources

InvestorGain — subscription and GMP data across 1,380 IPOs from 2019 to 2026. IPOCentral — historical listing performance data.
